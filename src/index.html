Technology

Front End:

  • Vue framework (v2 and v3 composition API)
  • Vuex and Nuxt experience
  • AngularJS framework
  • Aurelia framework
  • Ember framework
  • HTML/HTML5 and CSS/CSS3
  • Templating engines such as Handlebars, Mustache and Gulp
  • Axios
  • PhaserJS (v2, some experimentation with v3)
  • D3js(v3 and v4)
  • ThreeJS
  • MelonJS
  • WebXR and WebAR
  • PlayCanvas engine
  • SVG and Canvas animation
  • Lowlag and Howler
  • PixiJS
  • ModelViewerJS
  • Electron

  • Microsoft Azure deployments
  • Coldfusion 9 and 10 templating and project maintenance

Other:

About Me

I am currently a front-end focused developer with back end experience who initially entered the discipline working on a home grown LMS system and platform. I am passionate about the use of interactive assets, games and designs in order to help facilitate the teaching of complex subject matter (the primary focus of the platform in question was sciences, focusing on biology).

I have found myself throughout my career in situations where I would have to learn new technologies quickly on a project by project basis, and consider my greatest strength to be adaptability and the ability to troubleshoot or debug unexpected behaviours. I hope to draw from these experiences to provide value in being able to quickly adopt tech stacks as required, and to help in solving issues if they arise.

I also helped initialize, acted as production lead and then moved into a technical support role for the e-Learning division of my current company, where I put together a team of e-learning designers and developers, mentored in SCORM API and xAPI, and taught the use of and solved for extended features with Articulate Storyline.

I also have adopted many technologies for rich interactive web experiences, such as the Phaser game engine(v3), PlayCanvas engine, MelonJS, and have worked with rendering technologies such as PixiJS and ThreeJS. I am passionate about good game design and providing an interesting and engaging experience to users.

Technology

Front End:

  • Vue framework
  • Vuex and Nuxt experience
  • AngularJS framework
  • Aurelia framework
  • Ember framework
  • HTML/HTML5 and CSS/CSS3
  • Templating engines such as Handlebars, Mustache and Gulp
  • Axios
  • PhaserJS
  • D3js
  • ThreeJS
  • MelonJS
  • WebXR and WebAR
  • PlayCanvas engine
  • SVG and Canvas animation
  • Lowlag and Howler
  • PixiJS
  • ModelViewerJS
  • Electron

Back End:

  • NodeJS (project API's, data parsing and sanitization tools, and notably an application which leveraged NodeJS and Selenium to crawl internal web projects, screenshot them, and then produce weekly visual regression reports)
  • NestJS framework
  • mSQL and PostGreSQL
  • Axios with Firebase and Firestore deployments
  • Microsoft Azure deployments
  • Coldfusion 9 and 10 templating and project maintenance

Other:

  • Unity Game Engine (C#)
  • GameMaker (GMScript)
  • Articulate Storyline and Articulate Rise
  • Adobe Captivate
  • SCORM API and Tin Can (xAPI)
  • Python
  • Raspberry Pi and Arduino kits
  • Blender
  • Adobe Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, XD)
  • Audacity and Pro Tools
